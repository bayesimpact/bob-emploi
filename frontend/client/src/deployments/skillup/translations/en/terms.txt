## 4. <a name="section-4"></a>Description of the service

BOB is a platform developed by the association BAYES IMPACT. BOB's algorithm analyzes data from the job market as well as millions of job search paths in order to provide each person with a diagnostic of their job search.

### 4.1. Obtain a data-driven diagnostic
BOB is integrated with SKILLUP's platform, enabling SKILLUP users to complete the questionnaire on the BOB website and receive a diagnostic from BOB. BOB produces the diagnostic by studying the user's profile and the current job market to help them identify how to boost their job search. After receiving the diagnostic, users are redirected to the SKILLUP platform to receive resources to help them in their career journey, according to their diagnostic.
